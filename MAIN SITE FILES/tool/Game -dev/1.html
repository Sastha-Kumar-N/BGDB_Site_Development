Amino Acid Matching Game

Amino Acid Matching Game Rules

Welcome to the Genomic Matching Game! The rules are simple:

  • Click on a card to reveal the Amino acid sequence it represents.
  • Click on a second card to reveal its sequence.
  • If the sequences on the two flipped cards match, the cards stay face-up.
  • If the sequences do not match, both cards are flipped face-down again.
  • Match all pairs to win the game!
