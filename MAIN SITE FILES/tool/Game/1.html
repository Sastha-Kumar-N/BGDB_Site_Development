Genomic Matching Game Rules

Welcome to the Genomic Matching Game! The rules are simple:

  1. Click on a card to reveal the genomic sequence it represents.
  2. Click on a second card to reveal its sequence.
  3. If the sequences on the two flipped cards match, the cards stay face-up.
  4. If the sequences do not match, both cards are flipped face-down again.
  5. Match all pairs to win the game!
